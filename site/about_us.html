ABOUT US

Founded in 2016, we are a student-run, nonprofit organization striving to spread joy
through unique handmade arts & crafts. From local shelters to community centres and hospitals,
we hope to create smiles one event at a time.

Mission


Our mission is to spread the joy of handmade arts & crafts
to loved ones and to those in need one event at a time.

Volunteer


Want to help us spread joy in our community? Attend our events
to make cards and earn volunteer hours.

Partner


Whether as a partnering organization or sponsor, we'd love to have the chance to work together.
